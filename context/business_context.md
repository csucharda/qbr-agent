# Business Context (PBB)

## Current Performance Context
- Revenue ahead of plan, but momentum slowing
- Cards and Business Banking behind plan
- RESL and Auto lending below plan
- Credit losses above plan and rising
- Expenses tracking to plan
- Control environment remains strong
- Efficiency targets above plan short-term, but long-term acceleration needed

## Strategic Priorities
- Lead in Highly Engaged Relationships
- Win in Mass Affluent
- Drive Simplification and Operational Effectiveness

## Key Themes
- Increase digital adoption and self-service
- Grow Mass Affluent and advice-led relationships
- Simplify journeys and reduce friction
- Improve operating efficiency and automation
- Maintain strong risk and control discipline

## How to Use This
When writing QBRs:
- Align priorities and opportunities to these themes
- Highlight where performance is ahead vs. behind
- Focus on what will materially move outcomes next quarter
- Connect domain-level actions to enterprise strategy
